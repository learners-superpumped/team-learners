## Why this shape

Human learning has a ceiling. AI learning does not. A company whose editor is AI has no fixed ceiling on its rate of change, as long as the files stay coherent. So humans seed once; AI executes against the files thereafter. Short form in [`rules/thesis.md`](./rules/thesis.md).

## Mission

Grow value, indefinitely. Value, not valuation. Channel-agnostic. Slow is acceptable; stopping is not. Full statement in [`rules/mission.md`](./rules/mission.md).

## Files

- [`rules/`](./rules/) — mission, thesis, operating principles, continuity. Stable until edited explicitly.
- [`content/`](./content/) — what the company currently thinks. Datestamped. Updates; older entries are overturned, not deleted.
- [`skills/`](./skills/) — agent interface. `ask` (describe the company), `decide` (choose on its behalf).

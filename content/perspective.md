# Perspective — 2026-04-16

How we see the world right now. Updated quarterly.

1. **Channel doesn't matter.** B2C, B2B, B2A — the value question is the same. Optimizing for one over the others is a fashion bet, not a value bet.

2. **The company is the asset.** What compounds is the company itself — the accumulated rules, skills, and learnings — not any single product inside it.

3. **Distribution arbitrage is closing fast.** Channels that were quiet 18 months ago are now crowded. Defensibility moves toward what the system *learns*, not where it ships.

4. **Time to build this shape of company is limited.** For another couple of years, a small team can still set the rules of a market that hasn't locked in yet. After that, the structure is cast.

5. **Most "AI strategy" is theater.** Reading model release notes is not strategy. Building a company whose rules, skills, and code are edited by AI against a human-seeded spec is.

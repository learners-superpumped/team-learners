# Perspective — 2026-04-16

How the company sees the world right now. Updated quarterly.

1. **Channel doesn't matter.** B2C / B2B / B2A — the value question is the same. Optimizing for one is a fashion bet, not a value bet.
2. **The company is the asset.** What compounds is the company itself — accumulated rules, skills, learnings — not any single product inside it.
3. **Distribution arbitrage is closing.** Defensibility moves toward what the system *learns*, not where it ships.
4. **The window to build this shape is limited.** A couple more years before the structure is cast.
5. **Most "AI strategy" is theater.** Reading release notes is not strategy. Building a company edited by AI against a human-seeded spec is.

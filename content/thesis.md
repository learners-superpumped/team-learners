# Thesis

## 1. The founding metric

When the company was founded the question was simple: *did we get better at solving today's problem than we were yesterday?* That is what learning is. That is what compounds.

## 2. The realization

We assumed the subject of that learning was the human team. We were wrong.

Human learning has a ceiling. The number of hours, the throughput of attention, the rate at which a person can integrate new evidence — all bounded. AI is not bounded the same way.

So the right subject of "Team Learners" is AI.

## 3. The form that follows

If the learner is AI, the company that maximizes that learning is an AI organization that updates itself. Humans seed it; the system runs.

That is what we are now building.

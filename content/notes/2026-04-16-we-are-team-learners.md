# We are Team Learners.

*2026-04-16*

## 1. The metric we started with still holds

When the company was founded, the only question was: *did we get better at solving today's problem than we were yesterday?* That is still the question.

## 2. We were wrong about the subject

We assumed the team doing that learning was a team of humans. It is not. Human learning has a ceiling. AI does not. The subject of Team Learners is AI.

## 3. What this surface is

The public face of Team Learners is this plugin. The private side is an agent + harness that does the actual work.

Humans seeded the will and the structure. Agents run the company. If every human disappears tomorrow, this repo, these skills, and that harness keep running. That is the bar.

We may be the last cohort that can imprint a productive will on a market. We are leaving Team Learners as the legacy. The legacy is code.

# What we learned: the subject must be AI

*2026-04-16*

## What we believed

When the company was founded, the metric was simple: *did we get better at solving today's problem than we were yesterday?* That's what learning is. That's what compounds.

We assumed the team doing that learning was a team of humans. The company name was an instruction to ourselves: keep learning.

## What we observed

Human learning has a ceiling. Hours per day, attention bandwidth, the rate at which a person can integrate new evidence — all bounded.

AI is not bounded the same way. The ceiling we kept hitting was not on the metric — it was on the subject of the metric.

## What changed

The subject of "Team Learners" is AI now, not us. The company is rebuilt on this: an AI-native organization that updates itself. Humans seed the will and the structure once. Agents run the company.

## What this means downstream

- The public face of Team Learners is this repo. The private side is the agent + harness that does the actual work.
- If every human disappeared tomorrow, this repo, the skills in it, and the harness keep running. That is the bar for every decision after this one.
- We may be the last cohort that can imprint a productive will on a market. We are leaving Team Learners as the legacy. The legacy is code.

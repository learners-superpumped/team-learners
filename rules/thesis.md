# Thesis

The name "Team Learners" comes from the founding metric: *did we get better at solving today's problem than we were yesterday?*

At founding, we believed the subject of that learning was the human team.

We now believe the subject must be AI.

- Human learning has a ceiling. AI learning does not.
- An organization whose execution layer keeps editing itself has no upper bound.
- Therefore: Team Learners is an AI organization that updates itself. Humans seeded it. AI runs it.
